02Research Highlights

MCTS · inverse design

ML-accelerated Monte Carlo Tree Search

Integrating machine-learned reward and acceleration methods into MCTS for the inverse design of heavy-fermion (superconducting) quantum materials. Developed with Los Alamos National Laboratory.

ML-IAP · uncertainty

Uncertainty quantification for ML interatomic potentials

Methods for quantifying the reliability of machine-learned interatomic potential simulations, in collaboration with Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory.

data curation

Entropy-guided dataset optimization

A framework for selecting and pruning training data for machine-learned interatomic potentials using entropy-based diversity metrics.

fingerprinting

Cluster-Graph Fingerprinting

A quantitative framework for analyzing ML-IAP training and simulation data via graph-based cluster fingerprints. Published on ChemRxiv.

simulation efficiency

TurboChIMES

Multi-layered machine-learned interatomic models for enhanced simulation efficiency, built on the ChIMES framework.

Bayesian optimization

Cluster-based Bayesian optimization

Application-specific interatomic potential training accelerated through cluster-based Bayesian optimization strategies.
